¿And what is Kingdom Hearts?

Kingdom Hearts is a crossover of several Disney cartoon titles and characters from Final Fantasy game but being adapted to the universe of Kingdom Hearts.
The saga focuses on finding the friends of the main character, Sora, which leads him to travel several worlds where he meets Disney's and Final Fantasy's characters. It will be there where by many coincidences (or perhaps destiny), Sora and his friends will be immersed in the war of light against darkness.

Main characters

These are the main characters of the game, although there are many more.

Sora

Sora is the main protagonist in the Kingdom Hearts series, as well as a Keyblade wielder.

Riku

Riku is the deuteragonist of the Kingdom Hearts series. He is a Keyblade master that has the power of light and darkness, and is always ready to sacrifice himself to protect his friends.

Kairi

Kairi is one of several original characters appearing in Kingdom Hearts, and is also one of the main characters in the series.
